Get ready to Step It Up this summer!

The Step It Up summer prep programs are designed to help students from Kindergarten to Grade 6 build the academic confidence they need for a strong start in September. With targeted support in Math and Language, plus practical tips for each grade level, each 6-week program blends learning, skill-building, and fun. Whether your child is starting Kindergarten or preparing for Grade 6, these structured, one on one sessions help reinforce key concepts, develop foundational skills, and make the transition to the next grade smoother. Sessions via Cal Video, available in both English and French immersion options, with sibling discounts available.

Each program is grade-specific and includes separate Math and Language packages, with tips tailored for each grade level.
These programs are ideal for children:
  • In Alberta entering Kindergarten to Grade 6 in Fall 2025
    •  
  • Who could benefit from review, skill-building, or extra confidence before school starts
    •  
  • In either English or French immersion programs (language packages can be adjusted accordingly)
Each grade includes:
  • A Math Package (6-week focus on grade-appropriate numeracy skills)
    •  
  • A Language Package (6-week focus on reading, writing, vocabulary, and communication)

  • You can choose one subject or combine both for a full package at a discounted rate.

  • Most sessions are 1 hour, once a week per subject.

  • Each package runs for 6 weeks during the summer (exact dates vary based on when you book).
